## US Police Shooting
This project analyzes data related to recent US police killings, focusing on the topic of racism in America. The dataset includes demographic details (name, age, gender, race) of individuals involved, as well as information about each shooting or killing eventâ€”such as the date, location, manner of shooting, whether the individual attacked or had a weapon, indications of mental illness, whether the incident was recorded (e.g., police body camera), and if the suspect attempted to flee. Additionally, there is a category column describing the type of weapon used by the suspect. The project aims to extract insights and analyze patterns surrounding racism and police violence in the US.
